# FooCal - Food Calorie Calculator

A simple and easy-to-use food calorie calculator that helps you 
track calories from your favorite fast food restaurants like 
McDonald's and other popular franchises.

## Features

- Browse foods from popular franchises like McDonald's
- Search for your favorite fast food items
- Track your daily calorie intake
- Monitor your calorie goals
- Works on mobile and desktop

## Who Is This For?

Anyone who eats fast food and wants to stay aware of 
their daily calorie consumption — whether you are dieting, 
going to the gym, or just being health conscious.

## How To Use

1. Open the app
2. Search or browse your restaurant
3. Select the food items you ate
4. See your total calories for the day
